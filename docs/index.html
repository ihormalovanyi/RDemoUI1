01 · the start

La Pineda

Costa Daurada, Spain

Golden sand, a shallow warm sea, one long promenade. A few quiet days — and a route sketched on a napkin.

next: Narbonne · ~290 km north

02 · crossing the border

Narbonne

Occitanie, France · Rome's first colony in Gaul

Over the Pyrenees and into France. The Canal de la Robine, croissants at Les Halles, a Roman road in the middle of the square.

next: Carcassonne · ~60 km west

03 · the middle ages

Carcassonne

UNESCO citadel · 52 towers above the Aude

From afar La Cité looks like a film set — up close, even better. Double walls, and a sunset we had almost to ourselves.

next: Barcelona · ~300 km back south

04 · the finale

Barcelona

Catalonia, Spain

Back over the same mountains, somehow different now. Gaudí, the Gothic Quarter, vermouth at six — and a sea that feels like ours.

end of the route · ~650 km together
